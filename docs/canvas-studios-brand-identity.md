### The Signature Element - Paint Splash

#### Design Characteristics

1. **Organic**: Natural, flowing paint forms
2. **Asymmetric**: Never centered, always dynamic
3. **Unique**: Hand-painted, no two identical
4. **Bold**: Visible from distance
5. **Intentional**: Meaningful placement on each sneaker

#### Sneaker Paint Application

- **Coverage**: 15-30% of sneaker surface
- **Position**: Side panels, heel, toe cap
- **Layers**: Multiple colors possible, max 2-3
- **Finish**: Matte or semi-gloss leather paint
- **Protection**: Clear coat for durability
